Join us for engaging community events throughout the year. From creative workshops to seasonal festivals, there’s something for everyone at SunnySide Café.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I RSVP for an event?

Click the RSVP button under each event listing or contact us directly through our contact page.

Are there any fees for the events?

Most events are free, but some may require a small fee or donation. Check the event details for specific information.

What should I bring to an event?

Unless specified, just bring yourself and a good mood! Some workshops may require you to bring materials, which will be listed in the event details.
